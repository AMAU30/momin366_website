GDPR Compliance

Last updated: June 15, 2025

At Momin366, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This GDPR Compliance page outlines how we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

Momin366 acts as a Data Controller for the personal data we collect from users of our Service. We process your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR.

1. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the following lawful bases:

Contractual Necessity

When you use our Service, we need to process certain personal data to fulfill our contractual obligations to you, including:

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Providing prayer time calculations
  • Delivering core app functionality

Consent

For certain processing activities, we rely on your explicit consent, including:

  • Marketing communications
  • Location-based services (when not essential for core functionality)
  • Processing of special categories of data

Legitimate Interests

We may process data when necessary for our legitimate interests, including:

  • Service improvement and development
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

2. Your GDPR Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

You may request a copy of your personal data that we hold.

Right to Rectification

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You may request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.

Right to Restriction of Processing

You may request that we restrict processing of your personal data.

Right to Data Portability

You may request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

You may object to certain types of processing, such as direct marketing.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time.

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at:

Email: dpo@Momin366.com

We will respond to all legitimate requests within one month.

3. Data Protection Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:

  • Encryption of personal data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security testing and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Data minimization principles
  • Staff training on data protection

4. Data Transfers

As a global service, we may transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When we do, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Adequacy decisions for recipient countries
  • Other approved transfer mechanisms under GDPR

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • For the duration of your account (plus a short grace period)
  • As required by law (e.g., tax records)
  • To resolve disputes or enforce our agreements

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.
