User Policy

This policy outlines how we safeguard user information and ensure compliance with the standards set by Apple and Google Play. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe environment for our users.

Information Collection

We collect information to enhance your experience and provide better services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our application.

Data Protection

We implement various security measures to ensure your data is protected:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to your information.

User Rights

You have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to your data if it is inaccurate.
